Overwatch Summer Games 2017 has officially begun! This event includes a whole new slew of skins for the heroes. This years summer games looks more 'summerie' as opposed to last years olympic style skins.

Beginning today, August 8th, players can collect new loot and even pick up last year's Summer Games gear at a reduced price.

Also making a triumphant return is Lucioball! This huge fan favorite is back for the Games-- This is a fun afast-paced 3v3 match taking place in a beautiful new stadium in Rio de Janiero or on a brand-new pitch located in the Sydney Harbour Arena. Players can choose between two modes: Lúcioball and the all-new Copa Lúcioball, which offers a more competitive experience with placement matches, skill ratings tiers, leaderboards, competitive points, and more.

You can view all of the new skins Here. This event will run from August 8th - 29th. See ya'll in the games!
