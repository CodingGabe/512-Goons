That's right, you heard it here first, Overcooked: Special Edition launches for the Nintendo Switch this Thursday for $19.99/€19.99

Overcooked: Special Edition, developed by the genius' at Team17 features the Overcooked base game along with The Lost Morsel and Festive Seasoning DLC. HD Rumble is also supported. What's included? This:

  • 44 campaign levels which can be played solo or couch co-op with up to 4 players
  • 9 versus levels to play head to head locally with either 2 or 4 players
  • 22 different chefs to play as. Choose a human, racoon, dinosaur, French bulldog, robot or even a reindeer in your quest for culinary greatness!
  • A range of crazy kitchens to play across with different recipes to master – cook soup, burritos, burgers, fish and chips and more!

Overcooked includes a campaign mode that can be played solo, as well as local cooperative play for up to four players and a versus mode for two or four players and I am very excited about this one ever since Nintendo dropped that trailer during their Indie Direct.
